What is the problem?

A nonprofit organization called Red Feather Development Group is easing the hardship, often bringing wood and coal to help the Hopi and Navajo communities. The GoFundMe at https://www.gofundme.com/f/chizh-for-cheii-2020 uses the money they receive to purchase and distribute wood to the community. Another group, the Forest Service is helping by harvesting and collecting wood to help. Also, ordinary people are raising awareness about this issue, creating their own GoFundMe, creating videos, websites, and doing everything in their power to help out the Hopi and Navajo community.
